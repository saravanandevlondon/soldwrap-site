Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 June 2026

SoldWrap does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. Everything happens on your device.

No account, no tracking

SoldWrap has no login or account system. We do not use analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party trackers. We do not build a profile of you.

Your screenshots stay on your phone

When you choose a sales screenshot, SoldWrap reads the text from it (item names and prices) entirely on your device, using on-device text recognition. Your photos and the text read from them are never uploaded to us or to any third party. They are not stored by us.

What's saved, and where

The receipt cards you create and your handle are saved only in local storage on your device, so the app remembers them between sessions. This data never leaves your phone, and removing the app removes it.

No data shared with third parties

Because we don't collect your data, we have nothing to sell or share. SoldWrap makes no network requests that transmit your personal information.

Children

SoldWrap is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update this page and the "Last updated" date above.
